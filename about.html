Bihar COVID Help is a webpage created in April 2021. Our goal is to provide COVID-19 related Helpline contacts, Hospital Availability, Testing Center, Vaccination Center and other such relevant information for the people of Bihar state at one place from different government resources. Our approach is to act as a bridge between Service Providers and Public by compiling the contacts of Service Providers (Government / NGOs / Volunteers) on one page.


Disclaimer: We do not claim to be a Government Body / Health Expert or a Service Provider. This is just a family product in these tough times of COVID-19 to extend a helping hand for public at large.
